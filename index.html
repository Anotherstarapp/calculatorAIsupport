📋 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use CalculatorAI?

Simply open the app and start calculating. The interface is designed to be clean and intuitive — no setup required.

Is my data stored anywhere?

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. CalculatorAI works fully offline. No internet connection is needed.

How do I report a bug?

Please contact us via the email below with a description of the issue and your device model. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Which devices are supported?

CalculatorAI supports all iPhones running iOS 15 or later.
